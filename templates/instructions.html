    Navigating HomeMixers

  • Home Page
    From the home page search for recipes by typing terms into the search bar or clicking on Find Random Recipe, This may also be done on the Navigation Bar.
    Your user feed will appear on the home screen, containing the most recent reviews made by you, and users you follow.
  • Saving Recipes
    After making a search, a list of recipes will appear. Logged in users can save a recipe simply by clicking on the Save button. All saved recipes will be accessible from you Profile Page under My Saved Recipes.
  • Discover Users Page
    Find other users on *app_name*, view profiles, and follow/unfollow users.
  • Profile Page
    To View Your Profile click on the profile image icon in the Navigation Bar. Your Profile Page contains:
    • Profile Image, Banner Image, Username, and Bio (if present)
    • List of Reviews
    • Following link
    • Followers link
    • My Saved Recipes link
    • Edit Profile button
    • Delete Profile button
  • Write Reviews
    Write a review from your saved recipes by clicking on the Write a review button. The form to write a review will ask for:
    • A rating (from 0 to 5)
    • A written review (optional)
    • An image URL of your creation (optional)
    Reviews can always be edited or deleted by the user who wrote the review.
  • Cheers or Comment on Reviews
    Engage in other users' reviews by leaving them a comment or cheers'ing (liking) their review!
